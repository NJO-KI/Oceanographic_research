# Oceanographic_research
Oceanograhic research ,environmental monitering and marine ecosystem assessment.
Dataset Name: Bottle Water Sampling Data
https://www.kaggle.com/sohier/calcofi
Source: Oceanographic Research Database (NOAA/Research Vessel Sampling)

Rows: ~100,000+

Columns: 85 oceanographic and quality measurement variables

Business Context: Water quality monitoring and marine environmental analysis

Dataset Summary:

This dataset contains physical, chemical, and biological measurements from water samples collected at different depths across multiple sampling stations. It's used for oceanographic research, environmental monitoring, and marine ecosystem assessment.
